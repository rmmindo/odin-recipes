Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It’s creamy and rich, but made without cream—just silky egg sauce and flavorful cheese.

This dish is simple, comforting, and best served hot.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan or Pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated cheese in a bowl.
  4. Mix hot pasta with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in egg-cheese mixture and season with black pepper.
  6. Serve immediately.
