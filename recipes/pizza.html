Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the world’s most popular dishes, featuring a crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It’s versatile, fun to make, and delicious to eat.

You can customize your pizza with pepperoni, vegetables, or any toppings you like.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Oregano or basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  4. Add cheese and desired toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
