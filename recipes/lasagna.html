Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of melted cheese. It’s hearty, flavorful, and perfect for sharing with family or friends.

This dish can be customized with vegetables, different cheeses, or even a vegetarian filling.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking beef with onion, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
